\begin{solutionbox}

\textbf{A/B Testing} is comparing two versions of a webpage to determine
which performs better.

{\def\LTcaptype{none} % do not increment counter
\begin{longtable}[]{@{}ll@{}}
\toprule\noalign{}
Component & Description \\
\midrule\noalign{}
\endhead
\bottomrule\noalign{}
\endlastfoot
\textbf{Version A} & Original webpage (control) \\
\textbf{Version B} & Modified webpage (variant) \\
\textbf{Traffic Split} & Usually 50/50 random distribution \\
\textbf{Metrics} & Conversion rate, click-through rate, engagement \\
